安装解决方案 sharepoint Farm

Add-SPSolution -LiteralPath "YourSolution.wsp"
Add-SPSolution -LiteralPath "C:\WSP\DocumentsSP.wsp"

Update-SPSolution CIdentity "xxx.wsp" CLiteralPath "x:\xxx\xxx.wsp" CGACDeployment


安装（部署）解决方案 到应用程序  （到站点）

>Install-SPSolution -Identity "YourSolution.wsp" -WebApplication "站点地址" -GACDeployment
Install-SPSolution -Identity "Lists.wsp" -WebApplication "http://ecm.dahuaecm.com" -GACDeployment

更新解决方案

>Update-SPSolution -Identity "YourSolution.wsp" -LiteralPath "<path>\YourSolution.wsp" -GACDeployment -force

收回解决方案

>Uninstall-SPSolution -Identity "YourSolution.wsp"

删除解决方案

>Remove-SPSolution -Identity "YourSolution.wsp"

注意：删除解决方案前，先要收回解决方案。

获取已增加的解决方案

>Get-SPSolution

解除解决方案的部署锁定

>Remove-SPSolutionDeploymentLock
